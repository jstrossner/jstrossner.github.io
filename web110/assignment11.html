Assignment #11: Web Accessibility

Activity One: This activity went into depth as to what Accessibility means and the implementations into technology done so for those that are disabled. There are many aims that these technologies should address such as visual, hearing, mobility, and cognitive impairment. Many are software technologies on our everyday devices that are seen to benefit all but were made as a specific accessibility tool for those who require it to use the device. Some of these devices are head pointers, screen readers, braille terminals, and keyboard overlays. Some of these devices such as a screen reader can be a paid commercial product(like JAWS on Windows), a free product(like ChromeVox on Chrome), or a software that is built into your operating system such as VoiceOver on macOS.
